Project Management

Our philosophy of project management covers the management of all key components of a project such as Quality, Cost, Time, Contract, Organisation, and Risks through all phases of the project. You will achieve the best project result by implementing us from the very beginning so we can have a positive influence on your project already in the design stage, followed by the tendering phase and finally during the construction.


Engineering

Bencer unites a network of inventive and highly skilled building engineers and design specialists. Together, we are best known for helping to shape ambitious buildings. With world-class expertise - creative and technical - we help to realise buildings that are better because they are more useful and attractive to the people who own and inhabit them, more sympathetic to their context and more commercially sustainable.


Construction

Bencer Construction holds a leading position in engineering and construction of the major industrial and commercial projects for foreign and international clients in China. Our business activities are focused on China and other Asian countries. The company is well positioned with highly specialized units for different kinds of industrial production facilities such as General Manufacturing, Life Science, Food & Beverage, Chemical Industries, and Renewable Energies.


Consulting

Bencer offers a wide range of additional services to complete your tailor made project package. From initial investment advisory, technical due diligence up to green building consulting and LEED certifications, we are capable of covering all aspects to round up a comprehensive and carefree project realisation.


Quality Standards

EIP, "Excellence in Process", is our core philosophy, the standard of our work, and our internal quality practice. Bencer Construction developed the EIP program to set the highest quality standards in-house in order to provide our clients the best performance possible. Our internal daily and weekly based controlling system ensures the compliance of all team members with the internal EIP standards.
